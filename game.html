Game Development

FNAS

I developed a game called "Five Nights at Springtrap's" using C# in Unity. The game is set in a spooky and abandoned restaurant where the player is the night guard. The objective of the game is to survive five nights by destroying animatronic characters who are trying to harm the player. The game features a challenging gameplay experience, with a variety of jumpscares to keep the player on their toes. Throughout the development process, I utilized my skills in programming, game design, and problem-solving to create a polished and entertaining game. Developing this game was both challenging and rewarding. One of the biggest challenges was creating a game that was both fun to play and quite difficult. I had to ensure that the game mechanics were balanced and that the AI system for the animatronic character was realistic enough to provide a challenging experience for players. Debugging and fixing issues that occurred during the development process was also a time-consuming and sometimes frustrating process. However, the feeling of accomplishment when I finally solved a difficult problem or added a new feature to the game was incredibly gratifying and educational. *** Due to github's file size limit, the game is currently unavailabe to download due to its high storage content ***

Choice Adventure 2

Choice Adventure 2 is a game that presents the player with various choices, each leading to different outcomes that affect the player's progress towards escaping an island. The game was designed to be challenging and thought-provoking, with unexpected twists and turns that require the player to think critically and make informed decisions. Creating "Choice Adventure 2" was an early milestone in my journey as a game developer. Though it isn't as impressive as other games I have created I still chose to mention this game in my portfolio as it was one of the first games I developed using Python, and it taught me the importance of planing the code ahead, effective debugging, and the value of functions. Through this project, I developed a deeper understanding of programming concepts and techniques as I was still a beginner such as Tkinter and such, which I have since applied to other programming projects.
